Background

Who we are and why we exist.

International services and production is a private company founded by Nevermind Bonginkosi Mhlanga after recognizing the lack of opportunities in townships. The company provides training and job opportunities, especially for young people, and partners with public and private stakeholders to bring technology and services to underserved communities nationwide.

Vision & Mission

Vision

Fight unemployment across Africa by empowering people from disadvantaged backgrounds to earn an honest living through their gifts and talents, prioritizing education and steering youth away from crime.

Mission

Employ, mentor, brand, and train talented people from disadvantaged backgrounds so they become well‑groomed, competent professionals in the fields their talents lead.

Ethos

We regard people as our greatest asset and prioritize clear communication, sound planning, and flexible programming. Our management is caring and future‑oriented, and our commitment to this ethos drives consistent quality on every project.
